Challenges and Lessons Learned

One of the main challenges was ensuring proper alignment of the contact form labels and input fields. To resolve this, I implemented CSS Flexbox, allowing dynamic, consistent spacing across devices.

Another challenge was styling the progress bars dynamically. Initially, fixed pixel widths caused inconsistencies; transitioning to percentage-based widths improved responsiveness significantly.

Accessibility and Compliance

To ensure accessibility, I incorporated ARIA attributes and alt text for all images. The page also passed WCAG 2.1 Level AA compliance checks, ensuring usability for screen readers.
