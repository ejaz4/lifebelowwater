Donate to SeaGate

Donate to SeaGate to help us continue our mission to protect and serve the oceans. Your donations will help us keep our operations running and help us expand our reach to more areas.

SeaGate accepts a range of payment methods for your donations and a range of plans for your donations to ensure that you can donate in a way that suits you.

Our Donation Strategy, Simplified.

We've come up with a strategy to make excellent use of donations received from members and supporters like you.

Donations are handled differently from money that is provided to us from things like government grants, sponsorships and brand deals.

Acceptable Payment Methods

And as always, we accept a range of payment methods to make sure everyone has a fair chance at contributing towards our mission and our goal.

We support the following payment methods:

  • Credit cards via payment processor (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Debit cards via payment processor (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, mada)
  • Google Pay
  • PayPal
  • Aliexpress
  • WeChat Pay
  • Cryptocurrency (BTC and XMR)
  • Cash (mail with a note)
